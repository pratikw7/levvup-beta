Achievements
Click each CORE HABIT to refresh them. Core habits are habits which are essential to make progress in life.
They ensure the following:
You keep completing small milestones everyday (Tenacity).
Your body is fit enough to have plenty of energy to complete your daily milestones (Fitness).

Your mind is focused thereby enabling you to be efficient and not get distracted (Mindfulness).

You have a clear roadmap of where you are heading towards, this will help you stay on track (Journaling)

Tenacity: Increases by 10 (out of 100) once everyday if any task is completed on that day.
Fitness: Increases by 10 (out of 100) for every task which was described as 'fitness related'.
Mindfulness: Increases by 10 (out of 100) for every task which was described as 'meditation related'.
Journaling: Increases by 10 (out of 100) for a day if task described as 'journaling related' is completed.
